Welcome

At the Miami Beach practice of Dr. Adam N. Carson and Dr. Yuchen Sheng, our commitment and philosophy is to provide patients excellence in dentistry with the highest level of service. Our friendly professional team strives to make you feel comfortable and relaxed with every visit. Our main goal is to empower our patients with excellent oral health, dazzling smiles, and the charm, confidence, and quality of life that come along with them.

In addition to general family dentistry, we offer the latest technologies in preventive, restorative and cosmetic dentistry. We provide an extensive range of safe, aesthetic, completely natural looking solutions to discolored, misshapen or missing teeth. Services include complete restorative and prosthodontic care, cosmetic and elective dentistry, root canals, routine hygiene and periodontal treatment, emergency services, and minor oral surgery procedures.

Part of our commitment to serving our patients includes providing information that helps you make more informed decisions about your oral health needs. Please browse our website to learn about the services we offer and how we can improve your smile and maintain your oral health.

If you have any questions or would like to make an appointment, call us today!
