DR. YUCHEN SHENG

Dr. Yuchen Sheng completed her undergraduate studies at Florida State University, and then earned her doctorate in dental medicine with honors at University of Pennsylvania. She received residency training in general dentistry, aesthetic dentistry and dental surgery at Lehigh Valley Heath Network in Allentown, PA.

Dr. Sheng believes in providing comprehensive treatment for you to achieve your functional and aesthetic needs. She is a certified Invisalign® provider and has completed training in injectable aesthetics. She is a member of the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, and the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry. At the University of Pennsylvania, Dr. Sheng received awards in both Radiology and Endodontics for outstanding achievement. To stay abreast of the latest technologies and techniques, she participates in continued education courses. Her passion about dentistry and gentle touch makes each patient’s dental visit a pleasant and educational experience.
